Who Are You?

Who are you? Sounds like an easy question to answer, right? "I am a girl who loves unicorns and pistachio ice cream." "I am a guy who's hardcore into metal music and vintage Camaros." "I'm a brainiac who gets excellent grades." Some "I am" things are obvious. Some are not. We ALL have hidden treasures inside us. And sadly, we ALL have demons inside that we don't want to suddenly come crawling out.we all have a dark side, but might be better kept to ourselves.
Remember this: No one is perfect.
